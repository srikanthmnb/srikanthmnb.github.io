A summary of my core technical competencies, instrumentation expertise, and data analysis workflows.

Technical Skills#

Advanced Characterization & Scattering#

  • Synchrotron X-ray Techniques: Small-Angle and Wide-Angle X-ray Scattering (SAXS/WAXS), Extended X-ray Absorption Fine Structure (EXAFS), Grazing-Incidence Small-Angle X-ray Scattering (GISAXS), Grazing-Incidence Diffraction, and X-ray Reflectometry (XRR).
  • Materials Characterization: Thermogravimetric Analysis (TGA), Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC), Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM), Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM), Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM), X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy (XPS), Dynamic Light Scattering (DLS), and Zeta-Potential.
  • Surface & Interfacial Science: Langmuir-Blodgett techniques, Axisymmetric Drop Shape Analysis (ADSA), Point of Zero Charge (PZC) titrations, and Vibrational Sum-Frequency Generation (SFG) Spectroscopy.

Analytical Chemistry & Core Instruments#

  • Spectroscopy Tools: Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR), Fourier-Transform Infrared (FTIR), Raman Spectroscopy, Fluorescence, and UV-Vis Spectroscopy.
  • Quantification & Automation: Inductively Coupled Plasma Mass Spectrometry (ICP-MS), Liquid Scintillation Counting (LSC), Karl-Fischer titration, and Automated Titrators.

Polymer Science & Engineering#

  • Synthesis & Rheology: Atom Transfer Radical Polymerization (ATRP), Surface-Initiated ATRP (SI-ATRP), Gel Permeation Chromatography (GPC), and Rheometry/Rheological characterization.

Computational Tools#

  • Programming Languages: Python, MATLAB, OpenSCAD, and R.
